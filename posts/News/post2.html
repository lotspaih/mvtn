MVTN No Longer Active

January 10, 2022

Unfortunately, since the previous MVTN net manager has officially retired and no replacement net manager was found, sadly, the net is no longer active as of 2022-01-10.

If you have a need to pass traffic in the Miami Valley area it is recommended that you try the Tri-State Amateur Traffic Net.

TATN meets every evening at 20:00 (8pm local) on the OH-KY-IN Amateur Radio Society's 146.670 (- pl 123) repeater.
