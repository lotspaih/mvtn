KC8HTP Stepping Down as Net Manager

December 1, 2021

Long time Miami Valley Traffic Net Manager, Frank Brewster callsign KC8HTP, announced that he will be stepping down as the net manager effective December 31, 2021.

The Miami Valley Traffic Net thanks Frank for all his years of service and dedication to the net.

73 Frank!

The Miami Valley Traffic Net is currently considering interested licensed amateur radio operators who wish to assume the net manager duties and responsibilities. If you are interested, please contact Frank (KC8HTP) at the end of one of the December 2021 nets.
