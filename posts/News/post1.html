KC8HTP Stepping Down as Net Manager

December 1, 2021

Long time Miami Valley Traffic Net Manager, Frank Brewster, callsign KC8HTP, announced on the November 29th net that he will be stepping down as the net manager effective the end of December 2021.

The Miami Valley Traffic Net would like to thank Frank for his years of service and dedication to the net.

73, and see you next time, Frank!
